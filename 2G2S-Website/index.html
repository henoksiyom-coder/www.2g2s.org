Our Core Mission

The Sebeta Integrated Agro-Ecological Hub (2G2S) is dedicated to building sustainable, high-yield, closed-loop agricultural systems. By fusing modern dairy production with clean bio-energy recovery and local aquaculture ecosystems, we create a thriving agricultural environment that respects nature and strengthens the regional food economy.

Our Integrated Operational Pillars

🐄

Dairy Processing Unit

Focused on high-quality dairy production, advanced calf rearing programs, and sustainable milk harvesting operations designed for maximizing livestock health and yields.

⚡

Biogas Energy Recovery

Converting farm waste into pure renewable energy. Our closed-loop bio-digester systems power operations while producing natural, nutrient-dense organic fertilizer.

🐟

Sustainable Aquaculture

Utilizing filtered, nutrient-rich water systems to cultivate local fish species, maximizing efficient space use and diversifying regional food production pathways.

🌾

Farmer Training Hub

Empowering local community agriculturalists with hands-on technical skills, modern ecological workflows, and collaborative cooperative business frameworks.
